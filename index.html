I'm Lloyd

After receiving a solid public education from 1985 to 1997, I skipped tertiary education and moved to London in 1999. Traveled around for a bit working on farms and in bars. Lived in Spain for a while then Portugal, travelled extensively in and around Europe and then back to Cape Town in 2002. Worked in Cape Town and then returned to London in 2004 for another more focused attempt at etching out a living for myself. Returned in 2006 and I've been in Cape Town ever since. No wife, kids, pets, piercing, tatoos, jewelry and a real love for the work I do. I am largely self-taught and continue learning and researching all the time.
